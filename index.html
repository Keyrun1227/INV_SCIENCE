Who We Are

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering welcomes you to be a part of the thriving computer science community and become visionaries and change makers of the future. The Department aims to produce quality professionals in the field of Computer Science and Engineering and strive for excellence in research and development. Our faculty has excellent academic knowledge and assist students to be dynamic in choosing and pursuing their interest. Innovative teaching and learning methods are used to achieve learning objectives through practice, exposure, and motivation. We believe that this approach to teaching-learning, coupled with practical experience gained during Industrial Training in reputed organizations, equip our students to handle the challenges posed by the software industry. The department takes the initiative to improve the skills pertaining to functional English, soft skills, analytical perspective towards problem solving in Computer science which serves them to improve their higher order skills of the students . All these are intended to nurture them to face the IT industry in a bold fashion.
